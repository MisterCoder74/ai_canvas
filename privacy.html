Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9th, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Vivacity AI Canvas ("we", "our", "the Service"), developed by Alessandro Demontis operating under Vivacity Design Web Agency, collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws.

2. Data Controller

Data Controller: Alessandro Demontis
Project Name: Vivacity Design Web Agency
Email: privacy@vivacitydesign.net

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Authentication Data (via Google OAuth 2.0)

When you sign in with your Google account, we collect:

  • Google User ID (unique identifier)
  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Profile picture URL

Legal Basis: Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) and Contract performance (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))

3.2 User Configuration Data

  • OpenAI API Key (stored encrypted, used only for your AI interactions)

Legal Basis: Contract performance (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))

3.3 Usage Data

  • Canvas state: Cards, positions, dimensions, connections, notes
  • Interaction history: AI tool requests and responses
  • Session data: Login timestamps, last activity

Legal Basis: Contract performance (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) and Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))

3.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics
  • We do not collect browsing behavior outside the Service
  • We do not share data with advertising networks
  • We do not perform profiling or automated decision-making

4. How We Use Your Data

Purpose Data Used Legal Basis
User authentication and account management Google ID, email, name, picture Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
Provide AI tool functionality API Key, requests, responses Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
Save and restore user workspace Canvas data, history Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
Maintain service security and stability Session data, login timestamps Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f))

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

All user data is stored in JSON files on our EU-based server infrastructure. Data is organized in individual user folders identified by Google User ID.

5.2 Data Structure

Your personal data folder contains:

  • config.json - Your API key and settings
  • canvas.json - Canvas cards and connections
  • history.json - AI interaction history

5.3 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • OAuth 2.0 secure authentication (no password storage)
  • Server-side session management with secure cookies
  • File permissions restricting unauthorized access
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Data may be deleted after 24 months of inactivity
  • Deleted accounts: All personal data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

7. Third-Party Data Processors

7.1 Google (OAuth Authentication)

Purpose: User authentication
Data shared: None (Google provides authentication token only)
Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

7.2 OpenAI (AI Services)

Purpose: AI-powered features (ChatGPT, DALL-E, Vision)
Data shared: User prompts and uploaded files (as initiated by user)
Note: Data is sent directly from our server to OpenAI using YOUR API key
Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU, you have the following rights:

8.1 Right of Access (Art. 15)

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)

You can update your profile information within the app settings.

8.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17)

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting privacy@vivacitydesign.net.

8.4 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)

You can export all your canvas data and interaction history using the "Export All Cards" feature within the app.

8.5 Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18)

You can request temporary restriction of data processing.

8.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3))

You can withdraw consent at any time by logging out and requesting account deletion.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR Art. 33 and 34.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on EU-based servers. Any transfers to third parties (Google, OpenAI) are protected by:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequate data protection frameworks
  • GDPR-compliant data processing agreements

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on the Service with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
